About Us

About Us --
Android Club is to facilitate the training and workshops to the students who are interested in App Development. This club also provides the projects and way to do the projects which are complex. The Training is provided by the external resource persons who will be very interactive and allow students to develop their own ideas into an app.

OBJECTIVES --
1) The main objective of the Android Club is to provide the basic knowlege of app development.
2) To develop Hands-on-Projects by the students who are interested.

OUTCOMES --
Students learn advanced app development. Students get certification from Google developers, App Brewery, etc. Real-time project experience.

MISSION --
Through training, the club will encourage the creation and growth of complex Android apps.

VISION --
To develop Android applications for the purpose of improving the overall quality of life in the community.
