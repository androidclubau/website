About Us --

Android Club is to facilitate the training and workshops to the students who are interested in App Development.
This club also provides the projects and way to do the projects which are complex.
The Training is provided by the external resource persons who will be very interactive and allow students to develop their own ideas into an app.

OBJECTIVES --

1)The main objective of the Android Club is to provide the basic knowlege of app development.
2)To develop Hands-on-Projects by the students who are interested.

OUTCOMES--

Students learn advanced app development.
Students get certification from Google developers, App Brewery, etc.

Real-time project experience.

MISSION --

Through training, the club will encourage the creation and growth of complex Android apps.

VISION --

To develop Android applications for the purpose of improving the overall quality of life in the community.


What we do here!

Imagine. Innovate. Impact.
That is our club's motto, which we make sure to induce into young minds associating with us. Android Club entertains a wide variety of activities to shape one's interest in Android platform like.

Workshops

We bridge the technological gap between the curriculum and the industry by conducting workshops with experienced professionals who have excelled in Android Development.

Hackathons

Unlimited hours of coding and a creative mind is the best combination for a Android developer. We provide many fast paced competitions to the students in order to up their game in the development field.

Training

We also provide training to the students interested in learning more about the Android development. We also aid and guide them in the projects taken up by them and also fund their publishing.
